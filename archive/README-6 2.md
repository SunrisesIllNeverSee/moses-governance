# Example: Governed Research Pipeline

## Scenario
A research team needs Claude to conduct due diligence on a market opportunity. Governance ensures methodology is documented and findings are verifiable.

## Setup
```
/govern research
/posture scout
/command depth deep
/command reasoning deductive
/vault load research-methodology
```

## Interaction

**Operator:** "Investigate the competitive landscape for AI governance tools."

**Claude (governed):**
> Research mode active — documenting methodology before execution.
>
> **Methodology:**
> 1. Define scope: AI governance tools for enterprise, agent-level governance, compliance-focused
> 2. Sources: public repositories, product documentation, patent filings, academic literature
> 3. Evaluation criteria: governance depth, audit capability, role hierarchy, enterprise readiness
>
> SCOUT posture — investigation only, no actions taken.
> Deep reasoning — following threads, not surface-skimming.
>
> [Comprehensive research with provenance tracking, source bibliography, evidence gaps flagged]
>
> Audit entry #55 logged. Methodology documented. Sources tracked.

## Value
Research mode forces methodology-first approach. SCOUT posture ensures pure investigation. Deep reasoning prevents surface-level analysis. Everything is auditable and reproducible.
