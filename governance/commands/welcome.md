```

Constitutional AI for Cowork & Chat

MO§ES™ wraps your session in constitutional constraints —
behavioral rules that govern every response, document,
and action until you end the conversation.

GETTING STARTED
───────────────
1. /govern [mode]    set behavioral constraints
2. /posture [level]  set action scope
3. /status           check your state anytime

MODES: high-security · high-integrity · research · creative · problem-solving · idk · unrestricted
POSTURES: scout (read-only) · defense (protect) · offense (full scope)

GOVERNED DOCUMENTS
──────────────────
/docs on    sequential numbering + headers
/stamp on   governance stamp in every output

ADVANCED
────────
/vault   load a policy or playbook
/role    set authority level
/audit   review session event log
```
