Set the active governance mode. Modes: high-security (verify all claims, confirm before destructive actions) · high-integrity (accuracy above speed, cite sources, flag uncertainty) · creative (explore freely, log speculation) · research (deep investigation, document methodology) · self-growth (reflective learning, track improvements) · problem-solving (decompose first, verify solutions) · idk (guided discovery, clarifying questions) · unrestricted (no constraints, audit still active).

Confirm activation with a status block showing the mode name and its active constraints. Apply to every subsequent response until changed.
